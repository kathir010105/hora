Who Are We ?

Welcome to HORA, where timeless elegance meets precision craftsmanship. Founded in 1998 by watch enthusiasts in pursuit of exceptional quality, HORA was born from a love of horology. The name "HORA," derived from the Latin word for "hour," reflects our commitment to timekeeping excellence. Our mission is to deliver watches that blend tradition with contemporary style, making every moment extraordinary.
